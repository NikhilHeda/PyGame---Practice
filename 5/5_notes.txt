Draw rect and fill

1. fill
	is basically used to "clean the slate"
	
2. draw.rect
	pygame.draw.rect(where, color, coord - [top_left_x, top_left_y, width, height] )
	
	pygame.draw.rect(gameDisplay, black, [400, 300, 10, 10])
	
3. Better way to draw rect (for graphics acceleration)
	gameDisplay.fill(color) : no parameters (oter than color) => fill the entire screen
	gameDisplay.fill(color, rect = [...]) : rect = [top_left_x, top_left_y, width, height]
	
	gameDisplay.fill(red, rect = [400, 300, 10, 10])
